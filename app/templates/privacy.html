OccupancyOS ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Service.

  • Email address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communications.
  • Password: Stored in encrypted form via Supabase authentication.
  • IP address: Collected at signup and during usage for security and fraud prevention.
  • Timestamp: Date and time of account creation and TOS acceptance.
  • TOS version: Version of Terms of Service you accepted.

1.2 Listing Data

  • Property titles and descriptions you submit for AI analysis.
  • Property type and target audience selections.
  • Amenity selections and preferences.
  • Audit scores, recommendations, and analysis results.
  • AI-generated content we produce for you.

1.3 Payment Information

  • License keys purchased through Gumroad.
  • Transaction IDs and purchase timestamps.
  • Credit/audit usage history.
  • Note: We do NOT store credit card numbers. Payments are processed by Gumroad.

1.4 Usage Data

  • Pages visited and features used.
  • Browser type, version, and operating system.
  • Device information (type, screen resolution, etc.).
  • Referral source (how you found our Service).
  • Time and date of visits and actions.
  • Session duration and interaction patterns.
  • Error logs and debugging information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service: Process AI analysis requests, manage accounts, and deliver features.
  • To improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and optimize performance.
  • To train AI models: Your listing data may be used to improve our AI algorithms and recommendations.
  • For authentication and security: Verify identity, prevent fraud, and protect against abuse.
  • For customer support: Respond to inquiries and troubleshoot issues.
  • For analytics: Understand user behavior and Service performance.
  • For marketing: Send promotional emails, feature announcements, and updates (you may opt out).
